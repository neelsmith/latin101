# *Possum*

Principal parts:

*possum, posse, potui, -*

(Active) infinitives:

present: *posse*, perfect: *potuisse*



Imperfect indicative

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| poterat | poterant |

Perfect indicative

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| potuit | potuerunt |


Imperfect subjunctive

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| potesset | potessent |


Pluperfect subjunctive

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| potuisset | potuissent |
