# *Sum*

Principal parts:

*sum, esse, fui, -*

(Active) infinitives:

present: *esse*, perfect: *fuisse*



Imperfect indicative

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| erat | erant |

Perfect indicative

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| fuit | fuerunt |


Imperfect subjunctive

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| esset | essent |


Pluperfect subjunctive

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| fuisset | fuissent |
