# *Sum*

Principal parts:

*uolo, uello, uolui, -*

(Active) infinitives:

present: *uelle*, perfect: *uoluisse*



Imperfect indicative

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| uolebat | uolebant |

Perfect indicative

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| uoluit | uoluerunt |


Imperfect subjunctive

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| uellet | uellent |


Pluperfect subjunctive

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| uoluisset | uoluissent |
